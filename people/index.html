Anatoly Soshilov, Ph.D.

Senior Toxicologist and Section Chief

Dr. Soshilov leads the New Toxicology Evaluations Section at OEHHA. He oversees the development and implementation of science-based chemical hazard assessments and works to advance novel toxicological methods for environmental health protection in California.

Dr. Bolstad is a Staff Toxicologist with expertise in chemical hazard evaluation and risk assessment. She contributes to NTES’s mission by developing toxicological evaluations and supporting evidence-based regulatory guidance.

Scott Coffin, Ph.D.

Research Scientist IV (Chemical Sciences)

Dr. Coffin is a Research Scientist specializing in chemical sciences and pharmacokinetics. He develops and maintains the OEHHA Data Explorer and PFAS pharmacokinetics modeling applications, supporting data-driven chemical risk assessment.
